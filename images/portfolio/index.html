Grapefruit

Built on Lava Rock!

Aloha, we're a punk/ska band from Hawaii with hurricane-like energy! Our music is influenced by The Minutemen, The Dead Milkmen, Operation Ivy, and The Violent Femmes. We've opened for bands like Green Day and Blink 182 and went on 3 US tours. Not too shabby for three guys from Kailua, O'ahu.

We would love to share our music with you and have all of our albums available for purchase on Bandcamp.com so check them out!

Perhaps you'd like to take a look at some of our performances from over the years! Chunka Chunka is of us opening for Green Day in 1996! Something a little more recent Grey Hair because we've been doing this for 30 years now!
